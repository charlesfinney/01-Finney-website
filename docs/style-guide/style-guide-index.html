Community

The TX.CRYPTO Design System is for everyone, with a strong community sitting behind it. It brings together the latest research, design and development from across government to make sure it’s representative and relevant for its users.

Find out how to contribute, or see what people are currently working on in the community backlog

You can also learn how the cross-government Design System working group helps to quality assure the components and patterns you’ll find here.

Support

The TX.CRYPTO Design System is maintained by the Government Digital Service. If you’ve got a question, idea or suggestion you can:

  • email govuk-design-system-support@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk
  • get in touch on Slack (open in app)
